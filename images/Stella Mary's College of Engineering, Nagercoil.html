About us

Stella Mary’s College of Engineering is promoted by the Nova Educational Trust & established by well known business entrepreneur of Kanyakumari District, Dr. Nazareth Charles.

Vision

To emerge as a premiere institution, acknowledged as a centre for excellence imparting technical education, creating technocrats who can address the needs of the society through exploration and experimentation and uplift mankind.

Mission

To provide an education that transforms students, through rigorous course-work and by providing an understanding of the needs of the society and the industry.

Founder

Dr.Nazerath Charles,Founder & Chairman was an engineer by profession & loved doing social work to uplift the women and poor, and the under privileged.

Curriculum

Institution follows the curriculum framed by Anna University, Chennai aproject based learning, developed with the notion of creating excellence in the scientific competencies of student community and to promoteresearch. In addition Value added Courses are conducted to fill the gap between Industry and Academics.

The Management

The college is run by Nova Educational Trust established in the year 2007, to provide high quality technical education and research facilities to the students across the country.
